Geography

  • Size: 142.71 square miles (369.62 km2)
  • County: Philadelphia
  • Geographic Center: 40° 0′ 34″ N 75° 8′ 0″ W
  • Airports: Philadelphia International Airport - Northeast Philadelphia Airport
  • Major Waterways: Delaware and Schuylkill rivers
  • Average Temperatures: January 32.0° F - August 75.3° F - Annual Average 53.6° F
  • Elevation Low Point: 10 feet (3 m) above sea level
  • Elevation High Point: 445 feet (136 m) above sea level

The city of Philadelphia is located in southeastern Pennsylvania at the confluence of the Schuylkill and Delaware rivers.
