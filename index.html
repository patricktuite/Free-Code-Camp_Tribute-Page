Geography

  • Size: 142.71 square miles (369.62 km2)
  • County: Philadelphia
  • Geographic Center: 40° 0′ 34″ N 75° 8′ 0″ W
  • Airports: Philadelphia International Airport - Northeast Philadelphia Airport
  • Major Waterways: Delaware and Schuylkill rivers
  • Average Temperatures: January 32.0° F - August 75.3° F - Annual Average 53.6° F
  • Elevation Low Point: 10 feet (3 m) above sea level
  • Elevation High Point: 445 feet (136 m) above sea level

The city of Philadelphia is located in southeastern Pennsylvania at the confluence of the Schuylkill and Delaware rivers. The city is divided into several large sections including Center City, North Philadelphia, South Philadelphia, West Philadelphia, Northeast Philadelphia, and Northwest Philadelphia. Center city is structured in a grid pattern with five public parks and is home to central business district and is a densly populatated residential area. North Philadelphia is a large former industrial area, home to many transitional residential communities and the main campus of Temple university. South Philadelphia is traditionally know as a blue collar residential area. Known for a diverse immigrant community.
